Help Venezuela 🇻🇪

Following the recent earthquake in Venezuela, many families are facing very difficult times. Unfortunately, Venezuela does not operate under the same rules as other countries. For most cases, the more official the entity, the less trustworthy it is; while community-led initiatives are more reliable.

In the links below you will find our closest friends that have been working non-stop in Caracas and La Guaira buying supplies, cooking meals, organizing donations and transporting them directly to the shelters or the people in need. The initiatives listed here are the ones we can personally vouch for and trust during this time of misinformation.

Thanks to you, more donations can reach families and make a difference. You can also contribute by sharing this page.
